Some kids have a special condition called narcolepsy. It makes their brain have trouble staying awake during the day, even when they got lots of sleep at night!

Think of it like this: Your brain has a special "sleep switch" that turns on at bedtime and off in the morning. For kids with narcolepsy, this switch gets confused and keeps turning on and off at the wrong times!

ðŸ”¬ Brain Science Fact: There's a tiny part of your brain that makes special "wake-up juice" (scientists call it hypocretin). Kids with narcolepsy don't make enough of this special juice, so they feel super sleepy during the day.
